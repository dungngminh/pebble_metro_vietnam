## Description

Track Vietnam's metro from your wrist.

Metro VietNam follows the three operating metro lines — Cat Linh–Ha Dong and
Nhon–Cau Giay in Hanoi, and Ben Thanh–Suoi Tien in Ho Chi Minh City. Choose the
lines you ride in the phone settings, then pick an origin and destination on the
watch. A live countdown shows the minutes and seconds until the next train
reaches your station, with a little train running along the A → B track.

Highlights:

• Live countdown to the next train at your station
• Pick an origin → destination trip and see the ETA at every stop on the line
• Reverse the trip end-to-start with one tap
• Pin upcoming departures to your Pebble timeline, with a reminder before each
• Works offline once synced — schedules for all three lines are built in
• Made for Pebble Time 2: touchscreen gestures and buttons, animated UI

Note: Vietnam's metros do not publish a realtime/GTFS feed, so times are
schedule-based estimates from each line's operating hours and headway.
